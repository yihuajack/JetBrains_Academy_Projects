Workdays

Imagine that you are a director of your own company and have to make a calendar of workdays for your employees.

Using the switch statement, show "Yes, you should go to work" in the console.log if the value is between 1 to 5 inclusively. Otherwise, show "No, this is your well-deserved weekend!"


Sample Input:
1

Sample Output:
Yes, you should go to work
